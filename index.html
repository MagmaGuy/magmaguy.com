MagmaGuy's Plugins:

EliteMobs is a plugin made for survival & survival-like servers which by default adds dynamic bosses and events . Is it also possible to create your own or install pre-made Custom Bosses, Arenas, Lairs, Minidungeons, Adventures, Custom Events, Quests, Custom Models & more!
